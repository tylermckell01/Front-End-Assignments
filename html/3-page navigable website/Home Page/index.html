What is Bodybuilding??

~~~~~~~ Steroids? ~~~~~~~~~~~~~ Muscles ~~~~~~~~~~~~ Weights? ~~~~~~

Bodybuilding is an art. Basically the overall goal is to grow
muscles as big as you can, then become as lean as you
possibly can. Bodybuilding requires an immense amount of
discipline, consistency, and dedication. It requires a lot
of knowledge of nutrition and weightlifting.
